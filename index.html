About Dr. Prasad Biware

Where Compassion Meets Expertise

Welcome to the official website of Dr. Prasad Biware, a distinguished physician with an unwavering commitment to providing exceptional healthcare services. With a career spanning over two decades, Dr. Biware has established himself as a leading expert in Internal Medicine, earning the trust and admiration of countless patients.Dr. Prasad Biware embarked on his journey in the field of Internal Medicine in 2003. His dedication and vision led him to establish the renowned Shatayushi Hospital in Majalgaon taluka, Beed district. This 100-bed hospital, equipped with a state-of-the-art 10-bed ICU, revolutionized healthcare in the region by offering modern-day facilities previously inaccessible to the local community.
