About Us

Where Compassion Meets Expertise

Dr. Prasad Biware

Dr. Prasad Biware is a renowned physician specializing in Internal Medicine with a career spanning more than two decades. He has dedicated himself to providing exceptional medical care and has made significant contributions to the field of healthcare. He is the founder of Shatayushi Hospital, a 100-bed facility with a 10-bed ICU, known for delivering advanced medical services to the local community in Majalgaon taluka, Beed district.

Dr. Biware's holistic approach to healthcare is evident in his innovative initiatives, including the 'Diabetic Club,' which provides regular health check-ups to diabetic patients. His passion for complex cases, particularly in toxicology, has led to a remarkable zero mortality rate in over 1000 treated cases.
